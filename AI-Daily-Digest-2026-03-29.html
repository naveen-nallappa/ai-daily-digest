Bloomberg Embraces MCP to Close the Agentic AI "Productionization Gap"

Bloomberg announced it is adopting Anthropic's Model Context Protocol (MCP) — the emerging open standard for connecting AI agents to external data sources and tools — to bring agentic AI into production across its terminal and data services. MCP, originally developed by Anthropic and now donated to the Linux Foundation's Agentic AI Foundation, has become the de facto connectivity layer for AI agents, with OpenAI, Microsoft, Google, and now Bloomberg all announcing support. With Bloomberg on board, financial data infrastructure is now natively pluggable into AI agent workflows.

Why it matters

Bloomberg terminal data flowing directly into AI agents is a significant development for wealth management. Firms that build MCP-compatible internal tooling now will have a head start integrating with this expanding ecosystem.
